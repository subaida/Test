Upgrading from FusionMaps Evaluation

This section is meant for users who have implemented the Evaluation version of FusionMaps in their application, and are now upgrading to the commercial license.

There is no feature limitation/difference between the evaluation version and the commercial version of FusionMaps, but for the fact that the evaluation version has "InfoSoft Global" and "FusionCharts" stamped over all the maps. If you have been using the evalution of FusionMaps in your application and are now upgrading to a commercial license of FusionMaps, you'll need to do the following:

  1. Replace Evaluation version SWF with the new purchased SWF Files (present in Download Package > Maps folder).
  2. Clear your browser cache - very important step.

When you now view your application, you would not find any stamps on your maps.
